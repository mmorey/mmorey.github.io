In 2006 I co-founded an environmentally friendly brand of clothing geared toward action sports enthusiasts. All of the clothing was eco-conscious and was made from 100% organic cotton or a blend of organic cotton, hemp, and bamboo. We used water based inks that did not contain any harsh chemicals or resins. On top of all that, we used absolutely 0% sweatshop labor and the shipping packaging was as minimalistic as possible.

After 3 years of pursuing the "dream" we realized our dreams had changed. We decided to wrap up shop and move on to other things in life.
